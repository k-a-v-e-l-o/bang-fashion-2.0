✅

Return Policy

Returns accepted within 7 days of receiving your order. Item must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached and proof of purchase.

🔄

How to Return (Nationwide)

Returns via Paxi — contact us to initiate a return, we'll send a Paxi return label. Refund or exchange processed within 3–5 days.

Nearby? Contact Us Directly

If you're near Pretoria or in Gauteng, contact us to arrange a handover — no courier needed.

Non-Returnable Items
